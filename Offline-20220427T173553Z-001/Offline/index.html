M.Tech. Programme in Computer Science and Engineering



To enable students to pursue their dream of higher education, IIIT Guwahati is offering an M.Tech. programme in CSE with a curriculum designed to create industry-ready professionals. We invite students to join us on our exciting journey of learning.


IIIT Guwahati is an Institute of National Importance set up by an Act of Parliament. The Act authorises the Institute to offer degree programmes with the approval of their Board. UGC automatically recognises all degree programmes floated by IIIT Guwahati. IIIT Guwahati does not come under the purview of AICTE. AICTE therefore does not object to any degrees offered by Institutes of National Importance. The situation is the same for all IIITs under the Government, all NITs, and all IITs.

M.Tech. 2021 Admissions

IIIT Guwahati welcomes prospective students for admission into the M.Tech. CSE programme. This is an On-Campus programme where the studnets have to attend classes physically on campus. Physical presence on campus enables a student to be in an academic environment which results in a tremendous increase in learning outside the classroom. Fresh graduates are therefore encouraged to apply.


Important Information for Prospective Students

  1. The syllabi are designed focusing on AI, ML, and Big Data.

  2. The program is designed for students with different academic backgrounds.

  3. Different elective options in CS courses to supplement students with non-CS backgrounds.

  4. Complete placement assistance and guidance for higher studies.

* If the situation due to COVID does not allow students to come to campus, then the following will be the learning process.

  • Use of Learning Management System to conduct interactive online classes, archiving lectures, assignments, online discussions, etc.

  • Lectures and labs will be conducted online according to the Time Table.

  • The examination will be conducted online via proctored mode.
