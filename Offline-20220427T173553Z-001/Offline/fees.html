Fees

When admission is offered and accepted by a candidate, he/she has to pay the fee (details is below) using the online payment facility available on the website.

This is a self-financed course and all students have to pay the full fees as given in table below. Fees are payable before the beginning of every semester. There will be no increase in the fees during the programme even if there is an increase for new students.

Serial No. Items Periodicity Amount (in Rs.)
1 Tuition Fee Per Semester 1,00,000
2 Institute Caution Money One Time (Refundable) 5000
3 Hostel Rent Per Semester 12,500
4 Mess Charges (Per Semester) 4,000 Per month * 4 16000
Hosteller 1,33,500
Non-Hosteller 1,05,000

Please note that mess charges can vary depending on inflation and other factors. Mess charges will be charged extra during winter and summer vacations if students avail mess facilities. The mess advance collected will be adjusted against bills.

Financial Support

No financial support will be provided to the students.
