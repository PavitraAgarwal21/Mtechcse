Frequently Asked Questions for Online Programme

Q1. What will be the degree awarded at the end of the online M.Tech programme?

→ Master of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering.


Q2. Will online be mentioned on the degree?

→ No.


Q3. Is it possible for online M.Tech students to extend the duration of their degree?

→ Yes. The maximum duration to complete their degree is four years.


Q4. Is it compulsory to attend the lectures at the time of delivery for online students?

→ The lectures are archived and can be accessed any time. However, students are encouraged to attend at the time of delivery.


Q5. Will the institute provide the placement support for online students?

→ Yes, complete support will be provided.


Q6. What will be the syllabus for the interview?

→ Fundamentals of Computer Science courses.
