M.Tech. Programme in Computer Science and Engineering (Online)



Fees

When admission is offered and accepted by a candidate, he/she has to pay the fee (details is below) using the online payment facility available on the website.

Online students can take courses in a phased manner as per their availability. The student in an online programme can opt for a pay-as-you-learn mode, i.e., the fees for the student will depend on the number of credits taken.

The minimum credit requirements for an M.Tech programme is 120 credits. The charge per credit will be Rs. 3500. Students taking more than the minimum number of credits will not be charged for the extra credits. If a student registers for 30 credits in CSE, he/she has to pay fees of Rs. 1,05,000/-. Subsequently, if he/she drops a course of 6 credits, Rs. 21,000 will be carried forward to the next semester. If a student fails in a course, he/she will be charged for the course, but if he/she repeats the course or gives a supplementary examination, there will be no extra charge.

The students who opt for all the courses offered in a semester, have to pay the full fees as given below.
